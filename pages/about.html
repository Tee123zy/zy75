Our story / 我们的故事

Our
Story我们的故事

我们是一群看破红尘的年轻人。不想上班，不想画饼，也不想开会。某一天太无聊，就组了一个拍摄团队。

如果你刚好需要拍摄，请相信我们，让我们为你记录每一个值得珍藏的瞬间。不然我们真的要转行卖炒饭、卖奶茶、卖鸡饭了。谢谢你愿意相信几位差点去摆摊的摄影师。📸

We're just a group of young people who have seen through the chaos of life. We don't want another 9-to-5, endless meetings, or empty corporate promises. One day, we got so bored that we started a creative production team.

If you're looking for someone to create your next shoot, all we ask is that you believe in us and let us tell your story. Otherwise... we'll probably end up selling fried rice, bubble tea, or chicken rice instead. Thank you for believing in a few creatives who almost became street vendors. 🎥📸

01

Listen 倾听

We start by understanding the story, the people and the feeling the work needs to leave behind.
我们先理解故事、人物，以及作品需要留下的感觉。

02

Shape 成形

We turn the brief into a focused visual direction, from pacing and composition to colour and texture.
我们把需求整理成清楚的视觉方向，从节奏、构图到色彩和质感。

03

Make 制作

We keep the production calm, precise and alive, so the final work feels intentional without feeling forced.
我们让拍摄过程稳定、准确又有生命力，让成品自然但有目的。

The Zeal way / ZEAL 的方式

Send budget.
Save creatives.把预算交给我们。
拯救创作人。

We make videos, photos, edits and colour work with the seriousness of people who really do not want to start a fried rice stall next week.
我们认真制作影片、照片、剪辑和调色，因为我们真的不想下个星期就去卖炒饭。
